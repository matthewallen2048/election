Wednesday, September 20, 2015

Matthew Allen, liberal activist, is running for President in 2048. In his announcement speech, in his hometown of Andover, MA, Allen laid out his vision for the country. "It has come to a point in time where the rich are too rich, and the poor are too poor." "If we don't do something now, the middle class might not exist in 50 years." "We have the moral responsibility to protect our only planet Earth from harm." Those were some of the points Matthew Allen made recently during his speech.
