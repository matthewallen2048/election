Who is Matthew

Matthew Allen is currently a 15 year-old attending Andover High School, in Massachusetts. He is passionate about promoting liberal values, and has done that throughout his community. Left, is an image of Matthew advocating for gun sense laws in his home state of Massachusetts.




Supporting Dems

Matthew's got a long time to wait until he launches his campaign. Until then, he's campaigning locally for town, state, and federal officials. He wants to ensure a win for the Democrats in November and will spend his time to do so.

Wednesday, September 20, 2015

Matthew Allen, liberal activist, is running for President in 2048. In his announcement speech, in his hometown of Andover, MA, Allen laid out his vision for the country. "It has come to a point in time where the rich are too rich, and the poor are too poor." "If we don't do something now, the middle class might not exist in 50 years." "We have the moral responsibility to protect our only planet Earth from harm." Those were some of the points Matthew Allen made recently during his speech.
